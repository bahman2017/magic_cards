Privacy Policy for Magic Cards

Last updated: 09/30/2024

Welcome to Magic Cards. Magic Cards is an entertainment app designed to amaze and entertain you with a unique and magical card experience. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this Privacy Policy.

By using Magic Cards, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information that directly identifies you, such as your name, email address, or phone number.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information automatically when you use the app, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, manufacturer, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID).
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, features accessed, and interaction with content.
  • Log Data: IP address, time and date of use, and other diagnostic data.
  • Location Information: Approximate location based on your IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the app.
  • To improve user experience and enhance app functionality.
  • To monitor usage patterns and analyze trends.
  • To serve personalized advertisements through third-party advertising networks.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service, provide the service on our behalf, or assist us in analyzing how our app is used.

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use data about your device and how you use the app to provide personalized ads.

Data Collected by AdMob:

  • Device information and identifiers.
  • Usage data and analytics.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies.

For more information on AdMob's privacy practices, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

3.2 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect app usage data to understand how users interact with Magic Cards.

Data Collected by Firebase Analytics:

  • Number of users and sessions.
  • Session duration and app performance data.
  • Demographic information (non-identifiable).

For more information, please review Firebase's Privacy Information.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Third-party service providers may use cookies, web beacons, and other technologies to collect information about your use of the app and other websites and applications.

5. Security of Your Information

We value your trust in providing us with your information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Magic Cards is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information from our records.

7. Your Choices and Controls

7.1 Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings: https://www.google.com/settings/ads
  • Android Devices: Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads > Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."
